ROUTINE SKETCHBOOK

2018

I came home and the first thing I did was take my shoes off. Without hesitation, I realised that this action is my routine. I leave the house every day, which means I can document this action every day. It seems to me that the fastest and less time-consuming way is photography. I did not take photos on the phone because I wanted to make a more stylised project. Polaroid camera helped me with this. I had the opportunity to instantly get a finished photo to further position it in the album and watch the changes in the position of the shoes every day
